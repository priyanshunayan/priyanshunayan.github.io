Works

Tu-du List is a simple and elegant to-do list. It's main aim is to organise your to-do lists and make your life simpler. It is very simple to use. You just need to add all your tasks and mark it done to remove it from the list. It is built with mobile-first approach and also suits itself to the larger screens.

Thought Machine takes only one click to operate. When you click on 'new quote', a new thought appears. You keep clicking and it will keep bringing new thoughts. Yes, it stops showing new thoughts when you no longer need new ones. This thing can really cheer you up when low.
